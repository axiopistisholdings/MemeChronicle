## What Is MemeChronicle?

[MemeChronicle](https://memechronicle.com/) is a free, automated news aggregator dedicated entirely to meme coins and the broader meme cryptocurrency space. Every story is sourced from trusted publishers, summarized without hype, and published with full attribution — giving traders, researchers, and enthusiasts a single reliable place to stay informed.

> **No hype. No speculation. Just facts.**

## Features

- **Automated aggregation** — news collected continuously from trusted sources
- **Curated summaries** — clear, concise, jargon-free summaries with attribution
- **Top 10 meme coin prices** — live price table updated regularly
- **RSS feed** — subscribe at [memechronicle.com/feed.xml](https://memechronicle.com/feed.xml)
- **Light & dark mode** — user-controlled display preference
- **Progressive Web App (PWA)** — installable on mobile and desktop
- **SEO-optimized** — structured data (JSON-LD), sitemap, robots.txt, Open Graph, Twitter Cards
- **Mobile-friendly** — fully responsive, works on all devices
- **Zero paywalls** — completely free, always
