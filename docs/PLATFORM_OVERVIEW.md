## About MemeChronicle

**MemeChronicle** is a dedicated meme coin news aggregator launched in 2026 by **Axiopistis Holdings LC**. The platform provides automated, neutral, fact-based coverage of the meme cryptocurrency space — free, with no paywalls, no advertising bias, and no hype.

Every article is sourced from trusted publishers, summarized clearly, and published with full attribution to the original source.

---

## Mission

To give meme coin traders, enthusiasts, and researchers a single, reliable, and unbiased source of information — filtering out noise and delivering only verifiable, sourced news.

## Technical Highlights

- **Architecture:** Single-page application (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript)
- **Data:** Automated aggregation from trusted news publishers
- **SEO:** Full structured data (JSON-LD / NewsMediaOrganization schema), sitemap, robots.txt
- **PWA:** Installable on iOS, Android, and desktop
- **Performance:** Minimal dependencies, fast load times
- **Accessibility:** Semantic HTML, ARIA labels, keyboard-navigable
- **Design:** Ultra-minimal news theme, light/dark mode, responsive layout
- **Brand color:** `#50C878` Emerald Green
- **Brand font:** VT323 (retro display, wordmark only)
- **Social:** [Bluesky @memechronicle.com](https://bsky.app/profile/memechronicle.com)
- **RSS:** Available at [memechronicle.com/feed.xml](https://memechronicle.com/feed.xml)
